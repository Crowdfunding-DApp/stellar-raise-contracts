# How Stellar Addresses a Specific Challenge in Our Project

## Challenge: High Gas Fees and Slow Settlements on Other Networks

Traditional crowdfunding on blockchains like Ethereum suffers from unpredictable gas fees and slow transaction finality, making micro-contributions economically unviable.

## How Stellar Solves This

Stellar's Soroban smart contracts offer:

- **Low, predictable fees** — Each Soroban operation costs a fraction of an ETH transaction, enabling contributions as low as micro-amounts without fee overhead eating into pledges.
- **Fast finality (3–5 seconds)** — Backers see their contribution confirmed almost instantly, improving UX and reducing drop-off during the payment flow.
- **Built-in asset support** — Stellar's native token (XLM) and anchor-issued assets (e.g., USDC) can be used for crowdfunding without extra bridging or wrapping.

By building on Stellar, our crowdfund contract can serve underbanked communities where low-value cross-border contributions are the norm, aligning with Stellar's mission of financial inclusion.
